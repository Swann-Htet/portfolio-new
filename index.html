About Me

Hello There!

I am Swan Htet Originally from Myanmar Currently studying in Thailand I love exploring new Technologies

My name is Swan Htet, and I hail from Myanmar. I was born in Bago, Myanmar, and I am honored to be recognized as a junior developer in my country. My passion for computers ignited at the age of 12, leading me to pursue computer-related studies during high school. I was fortunate enough to win the first prize in Microsoft competitions in my high school, a milestone that solidified my determination to become a software developer in Myanmar.

Following my matriculation exam, I enrolled in a Basic Software Engineering class, embarking on a journey that eventually led me to pursue Advanced Software Engineering. Along this educational path, I collaborated with my peers to complete two projects, both involving Java and C#. Additionally, I dedicated nine months to a comprehensive Web Development course, where I earned a diploma and successfully completed numerous web projects.

My dedication to expanding my knowledge and skills prompted me to venture abroad for further studies. Presently, I am enrolled in the Digital and Communication Engineering program at Mae Fah Luang University in Thailand. I am enthusiastic about contributing to the field of software development and look forward to the opportunities that lie ahead.
